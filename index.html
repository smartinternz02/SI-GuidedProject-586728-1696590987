About Us

EduDash is a company that specializes in creating online education system dashboard for schools, colleges, and universities. EduDash provides a comprehensive and customizable platform that allows educators and learners to access, manage, and analyze their online courses, assignments, grades, feedback, and progress. EduDash also integrates with various learning management systems, such as Moodle, Canvas, Blackboard, and Google Classroom, to offer a seamless and unified experience. EduDash aims to enhance the quality and effectiveness of online education by providing a user-friendly and data-driven dashboard that supports learning outcomes and engagement.

What we do?

Some of the functionality of EduDash are:

  • Course management: EduDash allows users to create, edit, delete, and enroll in online courses. Users can also view the course syllabus, objectives, schedule, and resources. EduDash supports various course formats, such as self-paced, instructor-led, blended, and flipped.
  • Grade management: EduDash provides users with a clear and detailed overview of their grades for each course and assignment. Users can also view their progress, performance, and feedback. EduDash allows users to export their grades to other formats, such as PDF, CSV, or Excel.
  • Analytics and reporting: EduDash offers users with a powerful and interactive dashboard that displays various metrics and insights on their online education. Users can analyze their learning outcomes, engagement, satisfaction, and retention. EduDash also allows users to generate and share reports with others, such as instructors, peers, or administrators.

Dashboard

A dashboard is a way of displaying various types of visual data in one place. Usually, a dashboard is intended to convey different, but related information in an easy-to-digest form. A dashboard can be used for different purposes, such as monitoring business performance, analyzing data, or controlling a device or a program A dashboard can include different elements, such as tables, graphs, charts, metrics, and indicators A dashboard can also be customized to suit the needs and preferences of the user

Report

A report in a dashboard is a way of presenting data analysis and insights in a visual and interactive format. A report in a dashboard can help you to monitor your key performance indicators (KPIs), track your progress towards your goals, and explore your data to discover new insights

Data Visualisation

Data exploration and visualisation are two important steps in creating a dashboard that can provide insights and answers to business questions. Data exploration is the process of examining, cleaning, transforming, and summarizing the data to understand its characteristics, patterns, and relationships. Data visualisation is the process of presenting the data in graphical or pictorial forms, such as charts, graphs, maps, or tables, to communicate the findings and trends effectively
